Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity as Earth becomes uninhabitable. The movie beautifully explores themes of love, time, and survival.

Actors

The movie stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Amelia Brand, and Jessica Chastain as Murph. Their performances bring deep emotional connection to the story.
